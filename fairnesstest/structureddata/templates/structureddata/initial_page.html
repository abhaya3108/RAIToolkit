RAI Toolkit

RAID (Responsible AI Development Toolkit) is a framework for bias audit, transparency, and robustness for machine learning & deep learning developers, analysts, and policymakers to audit AI models for identifying biases, explain their models, and check for any Vulnerability to make informed and unbiased decisions around developing and deploying them.

This toolkit asses dataset/model against all RAI principles like Fairness, Transparency & Robustness etc. The purpose of this toolkit is to:

  • Identify all type of issues like biases (Sample Bias, Label Bias etc.)
  • Report all issues to user using collective reports via graph, charts, metrics.
  • Mitigate these issues to create reliable and trusted AI system.

Fairness

Fairness is area of machine learning that studies how to ensure that biases in the data and model inaccuracies do not lead to models that treat individuals unfavorably on the basis of characteristics such as e.g., race, gender, disabilities, and sexual or political orientation.
